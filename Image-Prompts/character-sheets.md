# Character Sheets

## Instructions
Intended for use with Nano Banana Pro, GPT 2.0, Seedream 5.0 Pro

## Concept
You created an image of an individual you like, now you need a more indepth photo of them to be used for video generation.

## Contents
- [Simple Sheet](#simple-sheet)
- [Multiple Angle Sheet](#multiple-angle-sheet)


## Simple Sheet
*Front, Back and Face Close Up*
```prompt
Character sheet of subject, three panels side by side, plain gray studio backdrop, thin dark dividers. Left: full-body front, head to toe, shoes in frame, standing straight, arms relaxed, facing camera. Center: full-body back, same pose, facing away. Right: close-up of face and shoulders, calm gaze.
```

## Multiple Angle Sheet
*Four Angles and Face Close Up*
```prompt
A professional high-fidelity photography composite sheet. Use the uploaded reference image to strictly maintain the subject's identity, skin texture, and clothing details. Style: Ultra-photorealistic 35mm RAW photography, sharp focus, natural skin pores, and realistic fabric textures. > Composition: Two horizontal rows on a clean, neutral studio grey background. Top Row (Full Body): Four full-body standing shots in a sequence: front view, left profile, right profile, and back view. Bottom Row (Portraits): Three close-up headshots aligned beneath the full-body row: front view, left profile, and right profile.
Technical Specs: Natural studio lighting with soft, realistic shadows that define form without looking flat. Shot on a 50mm lens to avoid distortion. Maintain perfect scale, head height, and alignment across all panels. No 3D-style shading; ensure the image retains the organic "noise" and micro-details of a real digital photograph. Output as a clean, sharp, print-ready layout.

High-resolution fashion model composite sheet, 35mm film photography. Top row: Four full-body editorial photographs arranged horizontally against a clean, neutral studio cyclorama background. The subject is in a natural standing pose: 1) Direct front view, 2) Precise left profile, 3) Precise right profile, 4) Direct back view. Bottom row: Three matching high-detail portrait close-ups, perfectly aligned. The subject must maintain identical features, hair, skin texture, and attire (exactly as seen in the reference photo) across all seven views. Focus on film grain, natural skin pores, realistic textile weave, sharp details, and imperfect photo quality. Studio lighting is bright, neutral, and consistent, preserving real shadows and highlight details (not flat lighting). Accurate anatomical proportions and clear silhouettes are required. Perfect alignment of full-body views and portraits across rows. Professional, print-ready 8k photograph.
```
