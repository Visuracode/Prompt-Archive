# Story

## Instructions
Intended for use with Nano Banana Pro, GPT 2.0.
- Aspect Ratio: [16:9]

Fill at minimum Project Name and Location

Example:
- Project: *Maldives Vacation*
- Location: *Maldives Island Resort*

This is the minimum to allow the image model to generate the rest based on search data.

Fill in addition items to detail as you desire.

Use the created image with your video model that references images such as Seedance 2.0 or Gemini Omni Flash.

## Concept
A storyboard to direct your video scenes in the video model.  
Storyboards allow you to reference a specific scene or generate multiple scenes at once with precision.
