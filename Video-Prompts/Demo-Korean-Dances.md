## Prompt 1
*Bubblegum Point Dance (idol-style)*
```prompt
STYLE: Authentic creator/streamer dance clip, shot on iPhone 16, NOT a cinematic music-video production — no professional grading, no gimbal-smooth moves.
DEVICE/OPTICS: iPhone 16 rear Fusion camera (48MP, f/1.6, 26mm equivalent), mounted on a small tripod at hip height, static locked framing.
SCENE CONTEXT: Vertical 9:16, ~12s streamer dance short, the kind of clip a Korean Twitch/AfreecaTV creator posts to Shorts/Reels.
SUBJECT: Young adult woman, straight dark bob with blunt bangs, gold shimmer cowl-neck camisole with thin chain straps, black high-waist shorts, delicate necklace — confident, playful, idol-cover energy.
SETTING: Seamless smooth gradient backdrop, pastel pink fading to lavender, zero props, zero furniture, clean streamer-corner setup.
LIGHTING: Soft ring-light wash, even on face and torso, practical creator lighting — no three-point cinematic setup.
FORMAT MODE: ONE CONTINUOUS STATIC TRIPOD TAKE, no hard cuts.
CAMERA: Fixed hip-up framing, subtle natural micro-jitter from the tripod surface, no handheld sway, brief autofocus settle at the very start.
HOOK: She's already mid-move at frame one — a sharp point-and-wink on the beat drop, no build-up.
PERFORMANCE: Bright, bouncy idol-style point choreography — crisp arm points alternating left/right, quick wrist flicks, a playful head tilt and wink to camera, small hip bounces on the downbeat, big genuine smile throughout.
PHYSICS: Hair swings naturally with head movement, gold camisole fabric catches light and shifts with torso motion, chain strap sways slightly.
COMPOSITION: Framed from hip to top of head, vertical 9:16, slight natural headroom looseness typical of a self-set-up shot.
BEAT BREAKDOWN: 0:00 mid-point pose on the beat — 0:03 alternating arm points with wrist flicks — 0:06 quick body bounce and hair flip — 0:09 final sharp point-and-wink held for a beat, natural half-second breath before the clip ends.
AUDIO: Upbeat K-pop-style dance-pop instrumental playing from her setup, phone mic picking it up with natural compression and light room bleed — no added studio audio layer, no spoken dialogue.
TECHNICAL: Native iPhone 16 video signature — natural sharpening/compression, sensor-shift OIS steadying the tripod shot without gimbal perfection.
CONSTRAINTS: 9:16 vertical, ~12s, tasteful high-energy idol-style choreography only — no explicit or sexualized body isolation. No cinematic color grade, no added score, no studio three-point lighting.
```

```prompt
HOOK: Clip opens already at full bounce energy on the drop — no build-up.
PERFORMANCE: Fast, high-energy bounce choreography — both arms pumping overhead on the beat, quick alternating shoulder bounces, a hair flip on the turn, genuine breathless laugh/smile at the peak, big open expressive energy throughout.
PHYSICS: Hair flies with the bounce and flip, camisole fabric moves with the arm pumps, natural momentum on each landing.
COMPOSITION: Hip-up vertical framing, 9:16, slightly looser/energetic framing consistent with big movement.
BEAT BREAKDOWN: 0:00 arms already pumping on the drop — 0:04 alternating shoulder bounces building energy — 0:08 hair-flip turn with a breathless laugh — 0:11 final big arm-pump held pose before the clip ends.
AUDIO: High-energy EDM/dance-pop track from her stream setup, phone mic capturing it with natural compression and a touch of room bleed, no dialogue.
TECHNICAL: Native iPhone 16 signature, OIS smoothing the worst of the bounce-induced shake without eliminating it.
CONSTRAINTS: 9:16 vertical, ~12s, high-energy but non-sexualized only. No cinematic grade, no added score, no studio three-point lighting.
```
